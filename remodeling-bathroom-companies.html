Do walk-in showers lower home value?

How long does tile last in a shower?

Ceramic or porcelain tiled showers can last approximately 60-80 years with proper yearly maintenance, including resealing the tiles annually and replacing the grouting every ten years or more if the shower experiences frequent use.

How much should a tub to shower conversion cost?

The cost of converting a bathtub to a shower as part of a bathroom remodel typically spans from $1,200 to $8,000, averaging around $3,000, and is influenced by factors such as size, accessibility features, fixtures, installation, and other related aspects.

What color bathroom increases home value?
